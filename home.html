About

Jeff and Karina, our sirens from Hell, are struggling to film their reality show that plays for the demons locked in Hell. Their location in the real world serves as a desolate backdrop, and they are unable to find a sinner to make an example of.

The two sirens set about to masterfully solve their problem and film the episode in the little time they have left before theyâ€™re sent back to Hell. With the use of duct tape and a magic camera, Jeff and Karina ingeniously create a dummy to fool their demonic audience. But is it enough to appease the creatures and allow them to continue on for another day in the real world?

About the Film

Sirens from Hell is a final project for a film production class at Queen's University. It is a commentary on the concept of precarious work, as well as the ideals of body image in today's society.
